Why France?

France is famous for its academic excellence, cultural heritage, and dynamic student life. Study in historic cities like Paris, Lyon, and Toulouse, and enjoy world-class cuisine, art, and architecture.

  • Prestigious universities and grandes écoles
  • Affordable tuition and living costs
  • Rich history, art, and culture
  • Central location for travel in Europe

Popular Universities

  • Sorbonne University
  • École Polytechnique
  • Sciences Po
  • Université PSL
  • Université Grenoble Alpes

Scholarships & Funding

France offers scholarships for international students, including government, university, and private awards. Explore options like:

  • Eiffel Excellence Scholarship Program
  • Erasmus+ Scholarships
  • University-specific scholarships

Student Life

Enjoy a vibrant student life with cultural events, museums, and festivals. France’s cities are known for their beauty, diversity, and strong support for international students.
