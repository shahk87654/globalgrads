Why Study in Canada?

Canada is known for safety, high education standards, and a welcoming environment. Top universities include UBC, McGill, and University of Toronto.

Top Universities in Canada

  • University of Toronto
  • McGill University
  • University of British Columbia (UBC)
  • University of Alberta
  • University of Waterloo

Cost of Living

International students typically spend between CAD 1,200 and CAD 2,000 per month on housing, food, transportation, and other essentials, depending on the city.

Visa & Work Opportunities

Canada offers student visa holders up to 20 hours of part-time work during semesters and full-time during breaks. Graduates can apply for a Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP) valid up to 3 years.

Student Life in Canada

With multicultural cities, friendly people, and stunning natural beauty, Canada offers a high quality of life. Students enjoy modern campuses, global connections, and safe environments.
