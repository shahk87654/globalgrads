Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes 2-4 weeks for university applications and 4-8 weeks for visa processing, depending on the country and program.

What documents do I need to apply?

Required documents include academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores, passport copy, personal statement, and letters of recommendation. Specific requirements vary by university.

Do you offer scholarship assistance?

Yes, we help students identify and apply for various scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities available for international students.

What is your success rate?

We maintain a 98% success rate for university admissions and 95% success rate for visa applications, thanks to our experienced counselors and comprehensive support.

Globalgrads Consulting helps students achieve their academic and career aspirations with expert guidance in university admissions, course selection, and visa applications.
