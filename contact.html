Your Questions, Answered.

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes 2-4 weeks for university applications. For visa processing, it can range from 4-8 weeks, largely depending on the specific country and program you're applying for.

What documents do I need to apply?

Generally, you'll need academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores (like IELTS or TOEFL), a passport copy, a compelling personal statement, and letters of recommendation. Keep in mind that specific requirements can vary significantly by university and program.

Do you offer scholarship assistance?

Absolutely! We actively help students identify and apply for various scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities specifically designed for international students. Our goal is to make your education abroad more accessible.

What is your success rate?

We pride ourselves on a high success rate: 98% for university admissions and 95% for visa applications. This is a testament to our experienced counselors and the comprehensive, personalized support we provide every step of the way.
