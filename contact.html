Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including university selection, application assistance, visa guidance, and scholarship search. Our goal is to provide end-to-end support for students aspiring to study abroad.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes, our initial consultation is completely free of charge. It's an opportunity for us to understand your academic goals and for you to learn more about how we can assist you.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out the contact form above, calling us, or sending an email. One of our counselors will get back to you shortly to schedule a time that works best for you.

Do you assist with visa applications?

Yes, we provide comprehensive guidance for visa applications, including document preparation, interview practice, and expert advice to ensure you are fully prepared for the process.
